About us

For the first time in Cluj-Napoca, 7 Gates Restaurant has brought the Open Kitchen restaurant and Fresh Beans Coffee varieties concept where you can enjoy a variety of dishes each prepared by the expert chef to give you the best dining experience. We provide you with a wide variety of dishes to choose from, each created with the perfection which you can enjoy with your family and friends in a peaceful environment.

An imposing space with a comfortable mezzanine with natural light where you can enjoy Lunch at any time of the day. We suggest you try the Grandma's Burger accompanied by a Mojito Cocktail made of strawberry and watermelon.
